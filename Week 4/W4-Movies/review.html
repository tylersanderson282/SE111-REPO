Interstellar is a breathtaking science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. In my personal opinion I believe this movie could be the realitly of our future. Matthew's performance as "Cooper" Makes the movie great as well, you can feel the authenticty within his character.


I would rate this movie a 9.2/10 solely for the fact that it was very well written with a major plot twist towards the end. The only downside in my eyes is the ending of the movie... I JUST WANT MORE! I highly encourage anyone who hasn't seen it... TO SEE IT!
